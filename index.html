Featured Game

THANKANS POOJYAM VETTU

THANKANS POOJYAM VETTU by Rikson Roy

A Dots and Boxes style game with local character.

THANKANS POOJYAM VETTU is made for entertainment and built around the familiar Poojyam Vettu / Dots and Boxes idea. It includes offline play, online room-based multiplayer, player progress, game settings, sound controls, themes, a store, and coin rewards.

Offline mode Play locally and challenge the Thankan-style game opponent.
Online rooms Create and join rooms with codes for friend matches.
Progress system Tracks wins and completion so players can keep improving.
Store and themes Includes coins, unlockable board themes, and sound settings.
